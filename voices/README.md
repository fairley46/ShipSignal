# ShipSignal Voice Library

Sample brand voices for ShipSignal. Each voice file is a drop-in replacement for
`config/voice.md` — or a starting point to blend with your own.

Pick one that fits your brand. Edit it freely. Or mix elements from multiple voices
into a single custom `voice.md`.

---

## The Voices

| Voice | File | Best for | Style in one line |
|---|---|---|---|
| The Operator | `the-operator.md` | Ops-heavy products, reliability platforms | Direct, accountable, metrics-first |
| The Visionary | `the-visionary.md` | Consumer products, design-forward platforms | Minimalist, poetic, human-centered |
| The Storyteller | `the-storyteller.md` | B2C, lifestyle brands, community products | Warm, authentic, failure-honest |
| The Customer Champion | `the-customer-champion.md` | Enterprise B2B, long-term relationships | Principled, clear, customer-obsessed |
| The Straight Shooter | `the-straight-shooter.md` | Developer tools, startup products | Raw, fast, zero corporate |
| The Connector | `the-connector.md` | Healthcare, education, HR, community | Empathetic, research-grounded, warm |

---

## How to Use

**Option 1: Replace voice.md**
```bash
cp voices/the-operator.md config/voice.md
```

**Option 2: Blend into your existing voice.md**
Copy specific sections — Core Rules, Banned phrases, Metric Translation — from any
voice file into your `config/voice.md`.

**Option 3: Use as reference**
Read through the voices to identify which patterns resonate with your brand, then
write your own `voice.md` with those principles as a guide.

---

## Notes

These voices are archetypes — communication philosophies distilled into actionable rules.
None of them are perfect for every situation. The right voice for your team depends on:

- Who your customers are
- What relationship they have with your product
- What your brand stands for
- How you talk internally

Start with the one that feels closest. Adjust until it feels like you.
